How I got here?

It was inspired by my personal international volunteering experience and how I found that my expectation and reality didn’t really line up. I wanted to create a platform where volunteers could be more prepared and inspired for their trip by reading other’s stories. To proceed, I conducted a semi-structured interview with 15 other volunteers. Then, I interviewed NGO in developing countries about their needs to gain an understanding of the covid context to aid future planning. From here, I synthesized the interviews to craft my mission and value statements.

Connect

I showed users in this demo how they would be able to connect with
one another and asked whether they would be interested in using this
feature. All of them gave positive feedback!

Filter & search

I tested whether users would prefer less or more options when they
search. Most would like few options to start the search with and have
more options in the filter available when browsing.

Competitive analysis

After comparing with the two leading organizations in international volunteering I claimed our niche in:

✅ Focus on community forming

✅ Pledge to focus on meaningful volunteering opportunities and design alternatives to voluntourism (mainly focus on alternative livelihood programs)

✅ We care about communities beyond international volunteers, specifically the communities that volunteers are devoting their time to
